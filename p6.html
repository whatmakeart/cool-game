Pacific Strike

This version tones down the shake, moves the guidance into a collapsible side panel, adds different aircraft sizes and speeds, thicker smoke and bomb effects, richer water and island textures, and structures that grow as islands become more powerful.

Launch feel
Small fighters streak fast, bombers lumber in, heavies hit harder, and some islands become true heavy-plane hubs.
Map control
Take factories early, fortresses at the front, fuel islands for reach, and heavy bases when you want giant aircraft swarms.

Victory

You control the skies.
