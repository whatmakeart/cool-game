Pacific Strike

This version uses generated SVG planes, bombs, impact splats, clustered air formations, and procedural sound. It is built to feel bright, splashy, and responsive on a phone.

Launch rhythm
Tap one of your islands, then tap any other island to send 50% of its planes in clustered squadrons. Tap the selected island again to launch a dramatic blitz.
How to snowball
Factories grow your air force, fortresses lock down fronts, and fuel islands sling squadrons across the map fast. The AI actively reinforces and counter-attacks.
