Pacific Strike

This version fully collapses the interface off-screen, removes whole-screen shake, adds island-only impact shake, improves shorelines and reefs, reserves space for the Help controls, and supports both drag launch and quick tap-to-chain attacks.

Controls
Drag from a blue island to launch 50%. Or tap a blue island to select it, then tap several target islands quickly. Double-tap the selected island for blitz.
Readability
Blue, neutral, and enemy islands are more clearly separated, numbers match ownership, water is continuous, and each island has more visible buildings and a harbor.

Victory

You control the skies.
