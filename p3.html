Pacific Strike

A punchier, smarter, more dramatic island air-war game built for mobile browsers. The map, effects, sounds, and island coastlines are all generated directly in the page.

Controls
Tap one of your blue islands, then tap another island to launch 50% of its planes. Tap the selected island again to unleash a larger blitz.
Strategy
Grab factories early, use fortresses as anchors, and fuel islands to strike across the map fast. The AI reinforces, counter-attacks, and hunts weak targets.
